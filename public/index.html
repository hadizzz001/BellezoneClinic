About Us

BelleZone Clinic was established in 2016 as a beauty clinic in Lebanon. We currently operate two branches in Lebanon, one in Koura district and another in Tripoli. We provide premium beauty services and products that enhance our clients’ physical appearance and mental relaxation. Our main target audience is men and women who are fashion conscious and interested in personal aesthetics. We sell a wide range of quality hair, and skin services, along with top lines of beauty products. We are committed to providing all of these services in convenient locations, superior quality, and high-end customer service.
